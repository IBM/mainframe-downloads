z/OS application modernization with IBM ADDI

Launch the course

https://learn.ibm.com/course/view.php?id=7680

Badge Overview

Digital credentials are the future and they are here for Application Discovery & Delivery Intelligence (ADDI). Digital credentials are transforming how IBM is engaging, recognizing and managing talent. The course is designed to provide the audience with an introduction to IBM ADDI, an analytical solution for z/OS application modernization.

Badge Description

The badge holder has successfully completed the self paced online course and zTrial Lab, covering the following modules:

Upon completion of this course, students will have the foundational knowledge on following topics:

  • Module 1 – Digital Transformation with ADDI
  • Module 2 – ADDI Product Overview and Key Use Cases
  • Module 3 – ADDI Key Graphs
  • Module 4 – ADDI Key Reports
  • Module 5 – ADDI Resource Usage in Jobs and Programs
  • Module 6 – ADDI & Crypto Discovery
  • Module 7 – ADDI Integration into a CI/CD Pipeline Overview
  • Module 8 – zTrial Overview & Workshops
  • Module 9 – Knowledge center reference and Badge Quiz
What it takes to earn this badge

Successful completion of the Application Discovery & Delivery Intelligence (ADDI) online self paced course and clearing the badge quiz assessment.

Please access the following URL to launch the course: https://learn.ibm.com/course/view.php?id=7680

You will receive your badge within 2 business days of completing the training curriculum. This badge can be obtained by any IBM employee or non-IBM employee.

More about the IBM Digital Badge program

IBM has established a Digital Badge Program to recognise our employees' significant achievements and skills attainment. IBM credentials are recognized, respected and valued globally in the IT industry. This program will enable you to easily and quickly share verified proof of your achievement both within IBM and externally. Where applicable, your IBM credentials will be represented by a digital image that contains verified metadata describing your qualifications and the rigorous process necessary to earn them.

Badge Queries

For questions about the DBB Basics badge program, such as a missing or incorrect badge or how to earn the badge, contact dat@ibm.com

Credly Support: For questions related to your Credly badge earner account and profile, as well as issues related to claiming your badge after receiving a notification, go to support.credly.com.

For Frequently Asked Questions about the IBM Digital Badge Program, click here.

Badge Privacy Statement

NOTICE: IBM leverages the services of Credly, a 3rd party data processor authorized by IBM and located in the United States, to assist in the administration of the IBM Digital Badge program. In order to issue you an IBM Digital Badge, your personal information (name, email address, and badge earned) will be shared with Credly. You will receive an email notification from Credly with instructions for claiming the badge. Your personal information is used to issue your badge and for program reporting and operational purposes. IBM may share the personal information collected with IBM subsidiaries and third parties globally. It will be handled in a manner consistent with IBM privacy practices.
The IBM Privacy Statement can be viewed here: https://www.ibm.com/privacy/us/en/
IBM employees can view the IBM Internal Privacy Statement here: https://w3.ibm.com/w3publisher/w3-privacy-notice
